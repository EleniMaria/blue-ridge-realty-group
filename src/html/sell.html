Seller's FAQ

Common questions, answered plainly.

How do you determine my home's value?

We use recent comparable sales in your specific town, current inventory levels, and any unique features (acreage, views, outbuildings) that don't always show up in automated estimates.

Should I renovate before selling?

Sometimes — but not always. We'll tell you honestly which updates are likely to pay off and which won't, based on what's actually selling in your market.

How long does it typically take to sell?

It depends on the town and season — mountain markets often move faster in spring and summer. We'll give you a realistic timeline based on current local data.

Do you handle land and unique properties, not just houses?

Yes — acreage, cabins, and unconventional mountain properties are a regular part of our business, and we price and market them differently than standard listings.
