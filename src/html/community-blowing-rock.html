Community Overview

Mountain luxury, village charm.

Blowing Rock is luxury mountain living at its most charming. Here, mountain living means stone-and-timber estates, a walkable village of boutiques and bistros, and some of the most photographed overlooks in the state.

Whether you're searching for a primary residence or a refined second home, Blowing Rock offers polish without losing its small-town soul — and our agents know exactly which streets carry that reputation.

Lifestyle in Blowing Rock

Schools

Watauga County Schools, with several respected private options nearby.

Attractions

Tweetsie Railroad, Moses H. Cone Memorial Park, and a walkable village of boutiques.

Dining

Upscale dining and bakeries lining a downtown built for strolling.

Outdoor Recreation

The Blue Ridge Parkway, Bass Lake, and some of the region's best overlooks.
