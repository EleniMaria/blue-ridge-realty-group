About Maddie

Maddie has spent over a decade helping buyers and sellers navigate the Asheville market, with deep ties to the River Arts District, West Asheville, and the Black Mountain corridor. A born-and-raised Asheville local, she grew up watching the city transform from a quiet mountain town into one of the most sought-after destinations in the South — and has the neighborhood-level knowledge that only comes from having lived through it.

Before joining Blue Ridge Realty Group as a founding agent, Maddie spent several years in residential development, which gave her an eye for construction quality and a candid perspective on what's worth paying for and what isn't. She's known for telling clients the truth even when it's not what they want to hear — and for finding the right property even when it takes longer than expected.

Outside of work, Maddie is usually at the farmers market, on a long trail run with her dog, or getting into a conversation she didn't plan to have at a bar she's been going to for fifteen years.
