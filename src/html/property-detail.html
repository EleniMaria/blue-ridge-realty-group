About This Home

Tucked into a quiet ridge above Blowing Rock, this four-bedroom mountain home pairs a classic stone-and-timber exterior with thoughtfully updated interiors. Vaulted ceilings and a wall of windows in the great room frame layered mountain views in every season, while the covered deck extends living space outdoors from spring through fall. The kitchen has been updated with granite counters and stainless appliances, and the primary suite occupies its own wing for added privacy. A short drive from downtown Blowing Rock's shops and restaurants, this property offers the rare combination of seclusion and convenience that defines the area's best addresses.
